Coloring Book

Color together with friends, in real time, on beautifully rendered paper. Bring your own pages or scan one with the camera.

↓ Download for macOS
macOS 14+ · ~2 MB · Free

Real-time collaboration

Add a friend, open a shared page, and see every stroke as it happens.

Five tools, hundreds of strokes

Sketch pen, color pencil, watercolor, crayon, pastel — each rendered with its own physics.

Trackpad as paper

One finger to draw, two fingers to move the pen. The trackpad is your canvas.

Bring any page

Drop in a coloring book PNG, scan a sketch with your webcam, or pick a built-in design.

Quick controls

Space
Toggle between pen mode and pointer mode
1 finger drag
Color (in pen mode)
2 finger drag
Move the pen without coloring (in pen mode), or pan the canvas (in pointer mode)
Pinch
Zoom in / out around the cursor
⌘0
Reset zoom & pan
Built with Swift, Metal, and a Node + SQLite backend.
